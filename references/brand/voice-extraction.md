# Voice extraction

## Reuse existing work verbatim
If the source assets contain a brand skill, a voice document, or a tone-of-voice section in a brand guide, that is the source of truth. Reuse it verbatim in brand/voice.md. Do not paraphrase or "improve" it. The client (or a previous Jiffi engagement) already did this work; rewriting it loses fidelity and authority.

## When no voice doc exists
Distil voice from the available copy (website text, brochures). Describe it as patterns: sentence shape, register, vocabulary, what they do and do not say. Produce do's and don'ts and a short list of approved phrases. Flag clearly that this was distilled, not provided, so the client can correct it.

## brand/voice.md structure
- Tone in one paragraph (who they sound like)
- Do's (concrete, with short examples)
- Don'ts (concrete, with short examples)
- Approved phrases / taglines
- Words to avoid
- Any language rules (Australian English, no em dashes, etc.) — these also go in CLAUDE.md non-negotiables
